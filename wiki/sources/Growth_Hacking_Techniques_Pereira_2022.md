# Growth Hacking Techniques — Daniel Pereira (2022)

## Summary

This BMA Super Guide presents growth hacking as a data-driven, iterative mindset focused on low-cost, high-impact tactics to rapidly scale businesses. It originated in the startup world but has become relevant across industries. The guide covers foundational elements (audience understanding, KPI setting, baselines), acquisition hacks (social media, SEO, paid ads, influencer marketing), retention and engagement hacks (loyalty programs, CRM, referral programs, email marketing), monetization hacks (pricing strategies, upselling, subscriptions), and viral/growth loops (social sharing, UGC, gamification). It concludes with case studies of Airbnb, Dropbox, Uber, Spotify, Slack, and Pinterest.

The core thesis is that sustainable growth comes from systematic experimentation across the full customer lifecycle—acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, and referral—rather than from isolated marketing campaigns.
