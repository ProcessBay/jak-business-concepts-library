# Go-to-Market Strategies (2nd Edition)

**Author:** Daniel Pereira  
**Year:** 2022  
**Length:** ~122 pages  
**Genre:** Comprehensive GTM framework

## Summary

Updated and expanded guide to go-to-market strategy covering: sales-led GTM, product-led GTM, market analysis and segmentation, product development and positioning, unique selling proposition (USP), value proposition design, and customer-centric product development. Includes practical methodologies for launch planning and execution.

## How JAK should use this source

- **Strong on:** GTM methodology comparison; market segmentation; USP development; launch planning
- **Weak on:** Digital-native GTM tactics; PLG mechanics; international market entry; regulatory considerations
- **Best for:** Launch planning; GTM strategy workshops; founder education on market entry
- **Not authoritative for:** Enterprise sales methodology; international expansion; regulatory compliance
