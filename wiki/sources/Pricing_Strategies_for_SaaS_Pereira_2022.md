# Pricing Strategies for SaaS

**Author:** Daniel Pereira (The Business Model Analyst, Ottawa, Canada)  
**Year:** 2022  
**Length:** ~85 pages  
**Genre:** Operational pricing guide for SaaS businesses

## Summary

A comprehensive practitioner guide to SaaS pricing covering target-market analysis, value-perception mapping, competitor pricing evaluation, cost and profit assessment, and seven pricing models (per-user, tiered, usage-based, flat-rate, freemium, value-based). The guide moves beyond model selection into monetization tactics—packaging, bundling, upselling, cross-selling, usage-based add-ons, white-labeling, and partnerships—and closes with startup-specific guidance on pricing experimentation, freemium/trial design, and renewal pricing for churn reduction. Case studies of Salesforce (tiered + value-based), Slack (freemium + per-user), and Zoom (simple tiered + per-host) anchor the theory.

## How JAK should use this source

- **Strong on:** Pricing model selection for SaaS; value-based pricing methodology; monetization tactics (upsell, cross-sell, bundling); startup pricing experimentation; renewal pricing and churn-reduction strategies
- **Weak on:** Advanced behavioral economics; enterprise negotiation tactics; regulatory pricing constraints; international tax and compliance implications
- **Best for:** Advising SaaS founders on pricing architecture; building pricing workshops for Utopian portfolio companies; evaluating pricing-strategy health for ProcessBay engagements
- **Not authoritative for:** Hardware/software bundle pricing; regulated-industry pricing (healthcare, finance); deep enterprise procurement dynamics
